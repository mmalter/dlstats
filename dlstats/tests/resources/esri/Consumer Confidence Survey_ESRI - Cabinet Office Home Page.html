Summary of the latest survey

The Survey of December 2015 was released on January 12th, 2016 New

  • This survey was conducted on December 15th, 2015.
  • It covered 8,400 households (Households of two or more persons are 5,712,One-person households are 2,688).
  • The response rate was 65.4% (Households of two or more persons:70.1%, One-person households :55.3%)
Page top

1. Consumer Confidence Index, Consumer Perception Indices

  • The Consumer Confidence Index (seasonally adjusted series) in December 2015 was 42.7, up 0.1 points from the previous month.
  • The categories of the Consumer Perception Indices (seasonally adjusted series), which are comprised of the Consumer Confidence Index in December are as follows;
        Overall livelihood: 41.1 (up 0.2 from the previous month)
        Income growth: 41.8 (up 0.7 from the previous month)
        Employment:46.3 (down 0.4 from the previous month)
        Willingness to buy durable goods:41.6 (the same as the previous month)

Page top

2. Price expectations a year ahead

  • The percentage of a group who expect "Go up" in December was 81.1%, a decrease of 1.0% points from the previous month.
  • The percentage of a group who expect "stay the same about 0%" in December was 11.1%, an increase of 0.9% points from the previous month.
  • The percentage of a group who expect "Go down" in December was 5.0%, an increase of 0.4% points from the previous month.
